About Us

Tamim Hassan Islamic Memorial Secondary School (THIMSS) is a beacon of academic and spiritual excellence established in 1995. We are committed to nurturing a generation of responsible, confident, and well-rounded individuals through a balanced blend of Islamic teachings and modern education.

Our qualified staff, disciplined environment, and student-centered approach ensure each learner receives the support and opportunities needed to thrive academically and personally.

Islamic Values

Instilling faith, discipline, and respect through Qur'anic principles.

Academic Excellence

Equipping students with critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Global Readiness

Preparing youth for success in a rapidly changing world.

Admissions

How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to join THIMSS:

  • Fill out the application form (available at school or online)
  • Sit for our entrance examination
  • Attend a family interview with the administration

Entry Requirements

  • A valid birth certificate
  • Latest academic records or report cards
  • Two passport-size photos
  • Demonstration of Islamic principles and behavior
